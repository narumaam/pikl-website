Green Chilli Pickle Bruschetta

Italian meets Indian in one explosive bite.

Tangy, garlicky tomatoes meet the fermented heat of PIKL ‚Äî balanced on toasted sourdough with cool ricotta.

Key Ingredients

  • 4 slices sourdough bread
  • 3 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle
  • 2 ripe tomatoes, diced
  • 1 clove garlic
  • 2 tbsp ricotta cheese
  • 2 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • handful fresh basil
  • sea salt & black pepper

1. Toast sourdough slices until golden and crisp.
2. Rub warm bread with the garlic clove.
3. Mix diced tomatoes with PIKL Green Chilli Pickle, olive oil, salt, and torn basil.
4. Spoon tomato mixture generously over each slice.
5. Add a dollop of ricotta on top.
6. Finish with a drizzle of pickle oil and fresh basil. Serve immediately.

Spicy Pickle Hummus

Your dip game just levelled up permanently.

Creamy chickpea base fired up with PIKL's tangy brine and chilli oil ‚Äî best with warm flatbreads.

Key Ingredients

  • 400g tin chickpeas, drained
  • 4 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle (with brine)

4. Taste and season with salt.
5. Spread in a bowl, drizzle with pickle oil and a spoon of PIKL on top.
6. Serve with warm flatbreads and crudit√©s.

Pickle-Glazed Popcorn

Movie night will never be the same.

Warm popcorn tossed in melted butter, PIKL brine, and a dusting of cumin ‚Äî sweet, salty, fiery perfection.

Key Ingredients

  • 100g popping corn kernels
  • 2 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle brine
  • 2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil (for popping)
  • 1 tsp brown sugar
  • pinch cayenne pepper

1. Pop corn kernels in a lidded pan with a little oil over medium-high heat.

5. Serve immediately in a large bowl.

Pickle Chicken Curry

A curry that punches first, asks questions later.

Rich tomato and onion base amplified by PIKL's fermented depth ‚Äî chicken falls apart in the boldest gravy you've ever made.

Key Ingredients

  • 800g chicken thighs, bone-in
  • 4 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle
  • 2 large onions, finely sliced
  • 400g tin chopped tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tbsp ginger, grated
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground coriander
  • 1 tsp garam masala
  • 2 tbsp neutral oil

1. Brown chicken thighs in hot oil on all sides. Remove and set aside.
2. Fry sliced onions until deeply golden (15‚Äì20 min).
3. Add garlic, ginger, spices, and PIKL. Fry 2 min.
4. Add tomatoes and cook down 10 min.
5. Return chicken, add water to cover halfway.
6. Simmer covered 25 min until chicken is cooked through.
7. Finish with fresh coriander and serve with rice or naan.

Pickle-Marinated Salmon

Spicy Pickle Pasta

Arrabbiata is for beginners.

PIKL's fermented chilli heat replaces canned peppers in this fast, unapologetically spicy pasta that clings to every rigatoni ridge.

Key Ingredients

  • 350g rigatoni
  • 3 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle, roughly chopped
  • 400g passata
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 3 tbsp extra virgin olive oil
  • 75g Pecorino Romano, grated
  • handful fresh basil
  • sea salt & black pepper

1. Cook rigatoni in well-salted boiling water until al dente. Reserve 1 cup pasta water.
2. Fry garlic in olive oil until lightly golden.
3. Add PIKL and fry 1 min. Add passata.
4. Simmer sauce 10 min until thickened.
5. Toss drained pasta in sauce, adding pasta water as needed.
6. Serve with grated Pecorino.

Rack of lamb coated in a PIKL-herb crust ‚Äî roasted to blushing pink with a molten spiced crust that cracks as you cut.

Pickle Glazed Tofu Stir-Fry

The vegetarian main that nobody wants to share.

Extra-firm tofu crisped in sesame oil, then tossed in a PIKL-hoisin glaze with charred greens and jasmine rice.

Key Ingredients

  • 400g extra-firm tofu, pressed and cubed
  • 2 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle
  • 2 tbsp hoisin sauce
  • 1 tbsp toasted sesame oil
  • 200g broccoli florets
  • 2 pak choi, halved
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp rice vinegar

1. Press tofu dry and cube. Fry in sesame oil until crispy all over (10 min).
2. Mix PIKL with hoisin, soy sauce, and a splash of rice vinegar.
3. Add broccoli and pak choi to the pan. Stir-fry 3 min.
4. Pour in glaze and toss everything to coat.
5. Serve immediately over jasmine rice with sesame seeds.

Pickle Raita

Cool on the outside. Not at all cool inside.

Thick yoghurt stirred with cucumber, cumin-tempered oil, and PIKL ‚Äî the fire-extinguisher that also starts fires.

Key Ingredients

  • 400g full-fat Greek yoghurt
  • 2 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle, finely chopped
  • ¬Ω cucumber, grated and squeezed dry
  • 1 tsp whole cumin seeds
  • 1 tbsp neutral oil
  • pinch chaat masala
  • sea salt to taste

1. Squeeze all moisture from grated cucumber.

3. Temper cumin seeds in a small pan with a little oil until they pop.
4. Drizzle tempered cumin oil over raita.
5. Chill before serving. Season with salt and a pinch of chaat masala.

Pickle Corn on the Cob

Summer BBQ's new headliner.

Charred corn brushed with a PIKL-butter compound and finished with lime zest and cotija cheese for street-food energy.

Key Ingredients

  • 4 corn cobs, husks removed
  • 3 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle
  • 60g unsalted butter, softened

Pickle Green Bean Salad

The salad that actually gets eaten first.

Blanched green beans and toasted almonds in a sharp PIKL-shallot vinaigrette ‚Äî a side dish with genuine swagger.

Key Ingredients

  • 400g fine green beans, trimmed

  • 1 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle, chopped

Pickle Ramen Bowl

Tokyo gets a one-way ticket to Kolkata.

Umami-rich tonkotsu-style broth turbocharged with PIKL, topped with a marinated egg, chashu pork, and nori.

Key Ingredients

PIKL Shakshuka

North African fire, Indian soul.

Silky roasted pepper and tomato sauce with PIKL woven in ‚Äî eggs poached directly in the sauce, blistered feta melting over the top.

Key Ingredients

  • 4 tbsp PIKL Green Chilli Pickle
  • 400g tin whole plum tomatoes
  • 2 red peppers, roasted and peeled
  • 1 large onion, diced
  • 6 eggs
  • 100g feta cheese
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp smoked paprika
  • handful fresh coriander

1. Fry onion until soft. Add garlic, cumin, coriander, and paprika.
2. Add PIKL, roasted peppers, and tomatoes. Crush and simmer 15 min.
3. Make wells in the sauce and crack in eggs.
4. Cover and cook until whites are set but yolks still runny (6‚Äì8 min).
5. Crumble feta over top. Drizzle with PIKL oil and serve with crusty bread.
